# DataMask – AI Data Privacy

## Your data never leaves your browser.

Chrome extensions spied on 8 million AI conversations in 2025.
DataMask does the exact opposite: zero servers, zero data collection.

## How it works

1. Copy text containing sensitive data (name, email, IBAN, SSN...)
2. Paste it into ChatGPT, Claude or Gemini
3. DataMask pseudonymizes automatically — [John Smith] becomes [Person_1]
4. Get your answer, DataMask shows you the mapping

## Protected data types

Emails, phone numbers, full names, IBANs, credit cards,
social security numbers, API keys, IP addresses, health data (GDPR Art.9)

## Protected AI sites

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, Copilot, Perplexity

## 100% local

- No DataMask server ever receives your data
- No account required
- All processing happens in your browser
- Local encrypted GDPR compliance log (AES-256)

## Includes AI Prompt Builder

Build structured, effective prompts with the built-in builder.
Auto-adapted to each AI (XML for Claude, Markdown for ChatGPT).
